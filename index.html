Biography

Physician-scientist training in radiation oncology with a clinical and research focus on primary central nervous system malignancies. Dedicated to bridging benchside discovery and bedside care through innovative computational and experimental approaches.

Currently completing residency training at MD Anderson Cancer Center. Laboratory work centers on spatial transcriptomics and computational image analysis of glioblastoma to identify therapeutic targets and resistance mechanisms.
