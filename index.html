Biography

Physician-scientist training in radiation oncology, with a clinical and research focus on primary central nervous system malignancies. Leveraging modern approaches to bridge benchside biological discoveries and bedside clinical care.

Currently at MD Anderson Cancer Center, interrogating the tumor microenvironment of glioblastoma using advanced spatial transcriptomics and computational imaging analysis.

Research Program

Dedicated to understanding therapeutic resistance in neuro-oncology through a multi-omic lens. Leveraging clinical data, tissue modeling, and computational methods to drive translational breakthroughs.

Spatial Tumor Microenvironment

Mapping intricate interactions between immune infiltrates and tumor cells following ionizing radiation using spatial transcriptomics.

Radiogenomics

Correlating non-invasive MRI features with genomic alterations to create predictive biomarkers for treatment response.
